Our goal 

Students 

We aim to smoothen the entire process for securing an internship for students in India. From starting your application till your first day at work, Quar.in helps bridge the gaps in three stages:

  • Apply for internships
  • Track their internship
  • Accept their internship

Companies

It can often be tough to reach out to aspiring applicants or perhaps have a aggregated database of potnetial interns to review systematically. Quar.in helps streamline this process providinng support in three simple steps:

  • Post their internships
  • Review their applicants
  • Send out acceptances
